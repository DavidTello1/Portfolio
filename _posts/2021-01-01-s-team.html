Description

Game made for the Grand CITM GameJam, the theme was 'women in STEAM jobs'. We were given 36h to complete the game and publish it to itch.io

S-TEAM is a 3D puzzle game made in Unity where the player must swap between five woman, each one with a power representing a field of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Mathematics). In order to solve the puzzles and complete the game, you will have to combine their skills, which are:

- Science: Change her physical size.

- Technology: Repair complex electronic circuits.

- Engineering: Push heavy objects.

- Art: Create stairs and door by painting them.

- Mathematics: Decipher hidden messages.

The game won the Best Technology Prize to the game with the best technical quality.


Role

I was one of three programmers of the project. For the design aspect of the game we did a brainstorming session to gather ideas and then we decided as a group what the final product would be.
