Role

This project was made for a university subject in a team of 2. We didn't have specific roles during the development of the project, instead we both did a little bit of everything. In the next section I have detailed the features I implemented for this project.

Features

Level Design

The game was required to be a 2D platformer with two levels. The levels were designed taking inspiration from Super Mario Bros games, //*** //*** - coins - grids - boxes - level transition

Entity System

The entity system is a factory class that manages the creation and deletion of all the entities and ensures they are updated accordingly. The entities are the following; - Player: the player controled by the user, including its movement and interactions: jump, wall-jump, dash and grip. - Coins: coins you can collect that give you points. - Grids: moving grids you can grip to advance the level. - Enemies: there are two enemies in the game, one is grounded and the other flies. Its AI system is very straightforward: if the player enters in its range it will persue them, if not just stay put. - Boxes: special boxes that are keys to advance the level, interconnected with the boxes in the other level. //***

Scene Manager

The scene manager is in charge of loading and saving the level data, and transitioning from one map to another. //***

UI System

The UI system is in charge of all the UI elements in the scene, used for creating all the menus and the HUD. //***

Music and SFX

Using SDL to manage the audio files, I added a theme for the main menu and another one for the levels. I also added some sound effects for the player interactions, like picking up a coin, jumping or dying. //***
